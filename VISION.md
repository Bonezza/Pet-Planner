# Vision

A digital home for every animal that matters to someone — not only animals they own.

## Profile types
- Pet
- Wild Friend
- Rescue
- Foster
- Memorial

## Adaptive dashboards
The dashboard changes based on the relationship and animal.

Examples:
- Turtle: feeding, water care, growth, UVB and Habitat Build Mode
- Dog: feeding, walks, vet, vaccinations and grooming
- Wild crow: sightings, trust, favourite foods, locations and behaviour
- Rescue: recovery, medication and progress
- Memorial: photos, stories, favourite things, timeline and tribute

## Habitat Build Mode
A Sims-style habitat designer with:
- moving, resizing and rotating objects
- real measurements
- collision boundaries
- saved layouts
- product search
- photo scanning
- future photo-to-3D objects

## Future features
- reminders and schedules
- timelines and memories
- health and growth records
- community habitat layouts
- smart habitat safety checks
- cloud backup and sharing

## Design
Dark, clean, modern, warm and personal. Original Purple and Heliotrope stay, with per-profile accent colours.

## Name
The final name is intentionally undecided. It should work for pets and wildlife, avoid implying ownership, and may eventually use an appropriate Aboriginal-language word with correct context and permission.
